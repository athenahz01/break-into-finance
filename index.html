The #1 Thing That Separates People Who Break In

It's not intelligence, pedigree, or connections. It's consistency of preparation. The people who break into finance from non-traditional backgrounds are the ones who:

  • Study the technical material until they can explain it simply (not just recognize the right answer)
  • Build actual models, not just read about them
  • Network strategically (quality over quantity)
  • Practice articulating their "story" until it's crisp and compelling
  • Treat the job search as a full-time job

This curriculum is designed to get you there. Work through it sequentially, complete every section, and you'll be better prepared than most candidates from target schools.
